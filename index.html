Guaranteed Authenticity

GIA & IGI Certified Diamonds

Every masterpiece at LuxJewel undergoes rigorous quality checks. Our solitaire diamonds are Laser-inscribed and accompanied by certificates from the Gemological Institute of America (GIA) or International Gemological Institute (IGI).

This ensures you are investing in a conflict-free, natural diamond of the exact Grade, Color, and Clarity specified. We believe in total transparency, which is why your purchase always includes an official appraisal document and a metal purity hallmark.
